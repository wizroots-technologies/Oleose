Forus, a portmanteau of “For Us”, is a technology-centric company that is focused on providing innovative, technology based solutions for health issues. Our name reflects our philosophy of providing affordable and accessible technological solutions in medical diagnostics to every socio-economic section of society.

Our flagship product 3nethra has been developed with the goal of eliminating the problem of preventable blindness in developing nations like India. 3nethra embodies our philosophy of employing innovation to make healthcare affordable and accessible to one and all. Our aim in devising 3nethra was to make diagnosis of eye problems a portable and cost-effective exercise.

Forus aims to democratize wellness by reaching out to rural and economically weaker sections of developing nations that do not have access to adequate eye care. Our portable diagnostic device, 3nethra has been used in more than 100 eye camps to screen over 2,50,000 people in rural areas for preventable eye diseases. Since its inception in 2010, Forus has grown in leaps and bounds into a globally recognized company, with 3nethra being used in over 14 countries. Our innovation, 3nethra, has been employed for screening more than 6 million eyes across the world. We believe in developing innovative products that can help the economically disadvantaged sector gain access to healthcare.
